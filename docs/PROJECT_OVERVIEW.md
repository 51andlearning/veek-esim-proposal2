# Project Overview

## What this is
A standalone proposal website that presents a **Travel eSIM** opportunity for **Veek** — Brazil's digital-first MVNO. The site lays out the strategy, business case, and integration approach for layering a Veek-branded, itinerary-aware travel eSIM on top of the existing Veek mobile experience.

## Why Veek
- Veek is positioned as "the only operator that offers FREE internet" — a brand built on simplicity, transparency, and a 100% digital activation flow.
- Veek runs as an MVNO on TIM's network with an existing base of **~5 million customers**.
- Outbound international travel from Brazil is a large, underserved opportunity for an MVNO whose customers already trust Veek for their primary line.
- A travel eSIM is a natural extension of the Veek app and the Gigacoins economy.

## Target audience for the proposal site
- Veek executive sponsors and product leadership reviewing the opportunity.
- MVNE partner stakeholders (commercial, technical, operations).
- External advisors invited to review the direction.
- Intended for authenticated link-sharing only (not public SEO).
