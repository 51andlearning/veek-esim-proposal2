# Scope

## Pages included
- `/` — Home / proposal landing (Veek-branded)
- `/strategy` — Strategic approach for travel eSIM inside the Veek proposition (placeholder)
- `/execution` — Execution plan and integration with the Veek app and Gigacoins (placeholder)
- `/appendix` — Assumptions, sources, model inputs, and references (placeholder)

## Sections included
The home page is composed of modular sections under `src/components/sections/`:
- Hero (Veek-branded)
- Executive Summary
- The Opportunity — outbound travel for the Veek base
- Why Travel eSIM (consumer benefits)
- The Veek Proposition — itinerary-aware, app-native, Gigacoins-integrated
- Business Case — 5M base, 10–20% penetration scenarios
- Financial Projections
- Customer Journey — Veek app, booking touchpoints, post-trip retention
- Integration Approach with the MVNE partner
- Timeline & Milestones
- Other Opportunities — adjacent revenue lines
- Call to Action / Next Steps
- Footer (confidentiality + Veek / MVNE marks)

## Explicitly excluded
- Creative production (in-app artwork, video, copy variants)
- Legal, privacy, and regulatory review deliverables (ANATEL, LGPD)
- Portuguese localisation of the proposal site
- CRM, billing, and Gigacoins systems implementation
- Customer-facing forms with live data capture (contact form is static for v1)
- Live analytics dashboards or BI tooling
- Procurement of eSIM connectivity partners (handled separately by MVNE)

## Launch milestones
1. **M1 — Scaffold complete:** Project created, shadcn primitives installed, docs drafted. *(current)*
2. **M2 — Content draft:** All sections populated with Veek-specific copy and the 5M / 10–20% business case.
3. **M3 — Veek stakeholder review:** Internal Veek review pass, feedback cycle.
4. **M4 — Deploy preview:** Published to a password-protected Vercel preview URL for joint review.
5. **M5 — Approved build:** Final content, tracking enabled, production URL live for Veek leadership.
6. **M6 — Post-launch:** Analytics review, iteration cycle, hand-off to commercial workstream.
